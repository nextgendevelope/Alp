Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Prompt2Pic

How much does Prompt2Pic cost?

Prompt2Pic offers a free tier with 20 generations per day.

Do I own the images I create with Prompt2Pic?

Yes! All images you generate with Prompt2Pic are yours to use for personal or commercial purposes with no attribution required.

What image resolution does Prompt2Pic generate?

Our free tier offers 1024x1024 pixel images.

Can I request specific art styles?

Absolutely! Prompt2Pic supports various art styles including photorealistic, anime, minimalist, watercolor, pixel art, 3D render, and many more. Simply include the style in your prompt or select from our preset styles.

How do I get the best results from my prompts?

For best results, be as descriptive as possible. Include details about the subject, scene, lighting, mood, colors, and art style. The more specific you are, the better the AI can match your vision.
